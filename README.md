# Habit Tracker

A simple, elegant, and privacy-focused habit tracker to help you build and maintain positive routines.

## User Experience

The app provides a distraction-free interface to track your daily habits.
- **Daily Logging**: Mark your habits as complete, failed, or skipped with a single tap.
- **Streak Tracking**: Visualize your progress with current and best streaks.
- **Calendar View**: Review your history with an intuitive monthly calendar.
- **Notes**: Add optional notes to any day to add context to your progress.

## Privacy & PWA

This application is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** designed with privacy as a first principle.

- **100% Client-Side**: There is no backend server. All your data is stored locally on your device using `localStorage`.
- **Data Ownership**: Your data never leaves your device. You have complete control and ownership of your habit history.
- **Offline Capable**: Works perfectly without an internet connection.
